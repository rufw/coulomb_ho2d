Detailed Description

Indices of a matrix element.

Here, n is the principal quantum number and m is to the angular momentum projection. In the form of a matrix element, the indices appear in this order: 

<n1, ml1; n2, ml2 | V | n3, ml3; n4, ml4>

where V is a two-particle operator.
